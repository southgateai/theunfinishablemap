### Deep Reviews

Comprehensive single-document analyses combining pessimistic and optimistic perspectives. These reviews:
- Identify logical gaps, unsupported claims, and counterarguments
- Find strengths and expansion opportunities
- Apply improvements directly to content
- Track review history via `last_deep_review` frontmatter

### Optimistic Reviews
